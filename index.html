Who We Are is What We Do

We are an award winning florist that combines classic and timeless arrangements with unique and creative designs, using the highest quality cut flowers to craft each arrangement to fit the occasion. Our large walk-in cooler is always filled with a variety of fresh cut flowers, bouquets, and arrangements to choose from! We pride ourselves on our wide variety of indoor plants as well as seasonal outdoor plants. We grow our own hardy mums in the fall, our own poinsettias for the Holidays, and during the spring we have our own geraniums, annuals and perennials, and hanging plant baskets. In our gift shop you can find locally made gifts and artwork, as well as candles, cards, jewelry, linens, holiday gifts, and more! We also have a large selection of air plants, succulents, and terrariums and all the supplies to build your own! We are open all four seasons, seven days a week.
